# Task 2 – LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Objective

The objective of this project was to identify and organize high-signal content creators and practitioners who actively use LinkedIn as a growth channel for B2B SaaS companies.

Rather than collecting a large volume of generic content, the focus was placed on selecting a small group of credible operators with proven experience in go-to-market strategy, demand generation, content marketing, outbound systems, AI-enabled growth, and SaaS pipeline development.

---

## Expert Selection Criteria

The selected experts were chosen based on the following criteria:

- Active practitioners rather than theory-only creators
- Consistent publication of actionable LinkedIn content
- Direct involvement in B2B SaaS growth, GTM, demand generation, or revenue operations
- Demonstrated expertise through companies built, clients served, products launched, or measurable business outcomes
- Availability of supporting content such as articles, guides, newsletters, videos, or transcripts

The final research set includes founders, operators, consultants, and growth leaders working across SaaS marketing, LinkedIn growth, outbound systems, AI-assisted GTM, demand generation, and content-led pipeline development.
