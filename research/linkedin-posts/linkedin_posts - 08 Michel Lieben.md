**Content/Summary/Key Takeaways**:

This LinkedIn strategy helps us generate $19,000+/day.

I helped a $10M/year SaaS founder replicate it in 73 minutes:

Here are some of the tips I gave him (that took me from 2K to 69K followers):

**1. Treat your profile like a landing page**

Your tagline needs social proof.

Your banner must include your value prop, not a vague quote.

**2. Get LinkedIn Premium for the custom CTA button**

One-click path from your post to your website.

No need to visit your profile first.

**3. Post at the exact same time every day**

Think of it like a TV show.

Pick a time you'll never miss and stick to it within 10 minutes.

**4. Never schedule your posts**

Always hit publish manually.

Scheduled posts consistently underperform. I tested it and saw an instant dip.

**5. Find 2-3 recurring formats you can decline every week**

Proven topic + proven format = consistent performance.

Content doesn't need to be new. It needs to reach people who haven't seen it yet.

**6. Build a swipe file of outlier content in your niche**

Use tools like Scripe or Taplio.

Filter for top-performing posts by specific creators, keywords, or adjacent niches.

**7. Mix "yolo" posts with ICP-targeted posts**

Personal storytelling brings people into your ecosystem.

But min. 2 posts per week must address your buyer's pain points directly.

**8. Carousels can 2x your impressions**

Images take up more space in the feed than text.

More visual real estate = more people stop scrolling.

**9. Repost your content 6 hours after publishing**

Delete yesterday's repost, publish today's piece, repost it by evening.

This alone adds 15-20% extra reach across different time zones.

**10. Repurpose your best hits every 2-3 months**

Slightly improve them each time. Add two more tools to your "top 10" list.

The biggest predictor of a post exploding is the same format exploding before.

**11. Reply to every comment in the first 30 minutes**

Engage for 15 minutes before posting to warm up your account.

Then stay on the post and respond to everyone immediately after.

**12. Expand the playbook to your team**

You can't publish 2+ posts/day without losing reach.

But 24 people posting added $151K MRR in 90 days for us.
